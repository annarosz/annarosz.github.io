Sunified
Presentation decks

An application of the brand identity, Sunified needed a variety of presentation decks for fundraising, investor relations, sales, events and internal documents.

Brand identity > Visual identity > brand strategy

The challenge

A sophisticated and accessible solution

Sumfried needed a wide variety of presentation decks, so the extension of the visual identity needed to strike a balance between consitency and flexibility. Content varies between text, images, technical illustrations, mapping, product, financial information, break out pages and more.

addition, a robust design solution needed to be created which could be implemented by a designer, but also by non-designers in the wider team. Templates were required in accessible formats like powerpoint and google slides.

The solution

KISS (Keep it simple stupid)

A more neutral version of our visual identity. A neutral colour palette with pops of colour. Simple does not mean boring, simple keeps the user in mind, it is cosidered and smart. Focus on high-contrast colours and other accessibility standards. Exporting assets in a variety of file formats. Modular sysytem, pages are interchangeable. Empower non-designers, demystify the design process. Brand guidlines and assets stored in a DAM, accessible to anyone on the team and any time.
