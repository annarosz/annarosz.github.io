Takeawei
Website refresh

An elevated e-commerce experience for Takeawei, a popular ceramics studio and lifestyle brand, bringing their online presence into alignment with their business goals.

Role > Freelance Design / Web Development

The challenge

An outdated e-commerce experience

Takeawei had an existing Shopify website that wasn’t functioning well and didn’t showcase their products and brand to the high standard that was required. Working within the limitations of the Shopify platform, my task was to design an updated user experience while migrating and respecting the existing backend structure.

The solution

A digital experience to support brand vision

Takeawei had a clear vision for their brand, a strong aesthetic and beautiful product photography. Working in collaboration, to keep business needs at the forefront, I developed a solution to showcase assets while not overpowering them, and to provide a premium digital shopping experience in harmony with the Takeawei brand.
