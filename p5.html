Takeawei – An elevated ecommerce experience for a popular lifestyle brand

Website / Shopify Development

WEBSITE DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

A digital experience to support brand vision

Takeawei had an existing Shopify website that didn’t showcase their products and brand to the premium standard that was required. Working within the limitations of the Shopify platform, my task was to design an updated user experience while respecting the existing site structure.

Takeawei had a clear vision for their brand, a strong aesthetic and beautiful product photography. Working in collaboration, to keep business needs at the forefront, I developed a solution to showcase products while not overpowering them, and to provide a premium digital shopping experience in harmony with the Takeawei brand.
