About

Hi, I’m Anna, welcome to my slice of the internet.

I’m currently in the midst of studying a full-stack web development bootcamp at Coder Academy in Melbourne.

Previous to catching the coding bug, I was working as a graphic designer for 12 years – in Melbourne, New York and San Francisco. After a few years finessing my craft in design studios, I took the plunge and started my own graphic design business and have successfully run that for the last 7 years. Before this I was working professionally as a visual artist represented by a contemporary art gallery in Melbourne as well as working on private commissions. I have a bachelor degree in both Visual Communication (Monash University) and Fine Art – Painting (RMIT University).

I have a particular interest in being able to combine art, design, technology and business to create products and experiences that enhance people’s lives. I’m excited to add development to my skillset and I’m eager to join the development community to learn, collaborate and meet interesting people doing interesting things. Technology plays such a big role in our lives so I’m keen to get involved and add my voice to the dialogue that shapes our future.
