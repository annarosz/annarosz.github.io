Sunified
Visual narratives

Sunified’s core brand identity was extended into secondary visual systems to help detangle complex business narratives and effectively communicate their products.

Role: Design lead

Marketecture (technical illustrations)

The challenge

Demystifying complex technical concepts

Translating complex technical concepts into a simplified visual form to clarify how the Sunified technology solution works. Striking a balance between technical accuracy and marketing to a wide (often non-technical) audience.

The solution

The power of visual communication

Translating and simplifying complex technical concepts into visual form making it easy for a non-technical audience to understand how products work. Bridging the gap between technical and marketing. A non-technical representation of products and systes and how they interact and relate to one another.
